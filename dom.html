Overview

The Dom is Zimmad's purpose-built solution to a problem that had dogged every Zeon ground-combat mobile suit fielded before it, the Gouf included: walking speed for a bipedal mobile suit tops out around 200 km/h regardless of frame strength, a hard physical limit no amount of leg redesign could solve. The Dom sidesteps the problem entirely with thermonuclear hover jets mounted in its feet, letting it skim across terrain rather than walk it — a genuine mobility leap over every prior Zeon ground unit, including the Gouf line it was developed to eventually replace.

Operational History

This archive's record of Dom deployment in the Southeast Asia theater is limited to a single unit dispatched from Ginias Sahalin's secret mountain base, encountered directly by Shiro Amada during the 08th MS Team's running campaign against the Apsalus Project. The engagement is notable in this archive's sourcing for an aside attributed to Shiro at the time, in keeping with a running joke across multiple generations of Federation pilots facing the type: a muttered reference to the Dom's distinctive hover skirt as looking like a "skirt" on an otherwise heavily armed and dangerous opponent.
