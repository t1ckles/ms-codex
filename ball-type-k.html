Overview

The Ball Type K is an early pre-production variant of the Federation's RB-79 Ball mobile pod line, superior to the later mass-production Ball in armor and propellant capacity alike. As a holdover from its origins as a civilian maintenance-pod design, it retained working utility equipment — lights, a rocket-guided cable winch — that was omitted from the simplified mass-production models that followed it. Its cockpit was militarized with a fire-control system, radar suite, communications gear, and a large tactical monitor, and its manipulator arms were enlarged compared to standard Ball variants.

Operational History

This archive's sole confirmed record of the Ball Type K in action is also one of the most consequential single engagements in the 08th MS Team's entire service history. Lacking any proper mobile suit aboard his transport, Shiro Amada launched in a stopgap Ball Type K to answer a distress call from a damaged Federation GM under attack by an experimental Zeon prototype. Using a piece of debris for cover, Shiro closed undetected, then fired the Ball's rocket-propelled winch to snag and entangle the Zeon unit, swinging the pod around to bring its cannons to point-blank range of the enemy cockpit. When the Zeon pilot drew a heat hawk to finish the Ball off at close range, Shiro held the blade back with the pod's manipulator arm just long enough to destroy the Zeon unit's head with cannon fire. Both machines were critically damaged in the exchange and the Ball was lost shortly after; Shiro escaped before it detonated.
