Overview

The Federation's standard-issue field jeep is unglamorous, unarmored, and utterly essential to the Kojima Battalion's day-to-day operations — the vehicle that moves personnel, light cargo, and dispatches between positions when committing a mobile suit or the hovertruck would be wasteful overkill. Its presence across this archive's Southeast Asia records is constant precisely because it's so unremarkable: background infrastructure rather than a combat asset in its own right.

Operational History

Jeeps appear throughout this archive's visual and incidental records of Kojima Battalion base operations and personnel movement in Southeast Asia, consistent with the vehicle's standard role as Federation ground forces' basic light-transport workhorse across every theater of the war.
