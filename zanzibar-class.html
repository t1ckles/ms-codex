Overview

The Zanzibar-class is a step up from the ubiquitous Musai-class in both size and firepower, typically assigned to fleet command duty or major offensive operations where the extra hull strength and weapons load justify the higher production cost. It's a less common sight than the Musai in routine deployments, reserved more often for operations significant enough to warrant a dedicated heavy escort.

Operational History

This archive's record of Zanzibar-class presence in the Southeast Asia theater is consistent with the class's standing role as a heavier command-tier escort, deployed in support of significant Zeon operations in the region rather than routine patrol duty. Specific engagement records involving the 08th MS Team directly remain limited.
