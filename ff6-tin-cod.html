Overview

The FF-6 TIN Cod is a small Federation patrol submarine, fielded as a coastal counter to Zeon's amphibious mobile suit and submersible transport activity in waterway-heavy theaters. Against a Southeast Asian front defined as much by its coastline and river systems as its jungle interior, an underwater patrol asset gives the Federation a means of intercepting Zeon's submerged logistics and amphibious assault routes rather than ceding that dimension of the theater entirely.

Operational History

TIN Cod-class patrol submarines are documented within this archive's Southeast Asia holdings as part of the Federation's coastal counter-presence against Zeon's amphibious and submersible logistics network in the theater. Direct engagement records involving the 08th MS Team specifically remain limited, consistent with the class's standalone naval patrol role.
