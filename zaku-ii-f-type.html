Overview

The Zaku II is the single most numerous mobile suit fielded by the Principality of Zeon during the One Year War, and the baseline from which nearly every Zeon ground variant encountered in the Southeast Asia theater — the J-type, the JC-type, and indirectly the Gouf line — ultimately descends. The F-type specifically refers to the space-optimized baseline configuration, prior to any of the atmospheric-mobility modifications that define its ground-combat derivatives.

Operational History

Within this archive's Southeast Asia holdings, the F-type baseline is documented primarily as the parent design referenced in technical records for the theater's ground-modified J and JC variants, rather than as a frequently deployed configuration in its own right — the jungle and mountain terrain of this front favored the ground-optimized derivatives almost universally. Federation intelligence treats sightings of unmodified F-type units in this theater as exceptional rather than standard.
