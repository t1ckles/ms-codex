Overview

The Jet Core Booster is a detachable thruster pack that gives a ground-type mobile suit short-duration atmospheric flight capability it doesn't otherwise have. Ground-type Gundams and GMs are built around terrestrial mobility rather than flight, so the booster exists specifically to bridge that gap for missions requiring rapid vertical insertion, aerial repositioning, or pursuit of an airborne or fast-fleeing target — circumstances the 08th MS Team encountered more than once given the Apsalus Project's flight-capable mobile armor.

Armament

  • NonePure propulsion attachment; relies on the host mobile suit's own armament

Operational History

Jet Core Boosters were issued to the Kojima Battalion's mobile suit complement on an as-needed basis for missions requiring aerial mobility beyond a ground-type unit's standard capability, consistent with the battalion's broader pattern of field-adapting standard-issue equipment to the specific demands of the Southeast Asia theater.
