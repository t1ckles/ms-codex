Overview

The Komusai is a small, lightly built Zeon transport, scaled well below a full Musai-class cruiser and intended for short-hop personnel and cargo movement rather than fleet combat duty. Its atmospheric capability makes it a practical option for ferrying personnel and light cargo into ground theaters without committing a far larger and more conspicuous warship to the task.

Armament

  • Light Defensive ArmamentMinimal; not built for combat

Operational History

Within this archive's Southeast Asia holdings, the Komusai is documented in a logistics role, consistent with its standard function across the wider war — moving personnel and light cargo rather than engaging directly in combat operations against the 08th MS Team or other Federation forces.
