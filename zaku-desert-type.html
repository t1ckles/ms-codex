Overview

The Zaku Desert Type adapts the standard Zaku II airframe for sustained operation in arid, high-heat environments — improved cooling systems, sand-resistant joint seals, and filtration upgrades to keep the suit's fusion reactor and actuators from fouling in fine particulate conditions that would degrade an unmodified unit's performance over time.

Per general Zeon order-of-battle records, desert-type Zaku units were fielded primarily in the war's arid and desert theaters elsewhere in the Earth Sphere. No engagement record connects this specific variant to the Southeast Asia front or the 08th MS Team's campaign.
