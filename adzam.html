Overview

The Adzam is a heavy tracked mobile armor built for sustained ground bombardment rather than mobile engagement, trading speed and maneuverability for armor plating and weapons payload well beyond what any conventional mobile suit chassis could carry. Its low profile and heavy frontal armor reflect a design intended to advance under fire rather than maneuver around it.

Operational History

Per general Zeon order-of-battle records, the Adzam was fielded as heavy ground-support armor on fronts where Zeon held a stable, defensible position. No record on file connects this unit to the Southeast Asia front or the 08th MS Team's campaign.
