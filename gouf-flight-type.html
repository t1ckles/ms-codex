Overview

The Gouf Flight Type adds genuine atmospheric flight capability to the Gouf line's existing close-combat specialization, trading some of the base Gouf's armor and ground-stability tuning for thruster output sufficient to bring the fight into three-dimensional space rather than a conventional ground engagement. Where the Gouf was already Zimmad's answer to the limitations of ground-modified Zaku units, the Flight Type takes that specialization a step further: a melee-focused mobile suit that can also dictate engagement altitude against opponents who can't follow it upward.

Operational History

This archive's record of the Gouf Flight Type's deployment in the Southeast Asia theater is limited to a single well-documented engagement: a confrontation with the 08th MS Team in which the type's flight capability brought the fight into open air rather than the team's usual ground-based combat, forcing the unit to fight in three dimensions for the first time in the campaign. The mobile suit proved a credible threat in that role, demonstrating the tactical value of altitude advantage against a Federation force built almost entirely around ground-combat doctrine.
