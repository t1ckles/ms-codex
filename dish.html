Overview

The Dish is a small, fast scout hovercraft used by Kojima Battalion forces for forward reconnaissance ahead of larger ground movements. Lightly built and minimally armed by design, it trades survivability for speed and a low silhouette, intended to gather information and withdraw rather than engage.

Armament

  • Light Machine GunSelf-defense only; not intended for sustained engagement

Operational History

Dish reconnaissance craft supported the Kojima Battalion's forward scouting efforts throughout the Southeast Asia campaign, feeding terrain and enemy-position intelligence back to battalion command ahead of larger operations involving the 08th MS Team and other ground elements.
