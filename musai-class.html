Overview

The Musai-class is the Principality of Zeon's standard light cruiser, the most numerous warship class in Zeon naval service and the backbone of its fleet operations throughout the One Year War. Distinctively designed with full atmospheric entry and landing capability, the class can deploy directly into a ground theater rather than remaining confined to space or orbital operations — a capability that made it directly relevant to Zeon's ability to project and resupply forces in a ground campaign as far from its primary holdings as Southeast Asia.

Operational History

Musai-class cruisers supported Zeon logistics and reinforcement efforts into the Southeast Asia theater throughout the campaign, consistent with the class's standard role as Zeon's general-purpose fleet workhorse across every front of the war. This archive's holdings on specific named vessels assigned to this theater remain limited.
