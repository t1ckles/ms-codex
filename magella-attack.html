Overview

The Magella Attack is Zeon's primary conventional armored vehicle for ground operations, built around the same chassis lineage as the earlier Magella tank but mounting a far heavier main gun in a fixed casemate configuration rather than a traditional rotating turret. It's a stopgap design philosophy — heavy direct-fire power on a relatively simple, fast-to-produce hull — that made it a useful complement to Zeon's mobile suit forces rather than a replacement for them, particularly in terrain or numbers where committing a full mobile suit wasn't justified.

Armament

Operational History

A trio of Magella Attack units volunteered for an ambush mission against the 08th MS Team during the team's operations in the Southeast Asia theater — a deliberately disproportionate engagement on paper, with conventional armor squaring off against Federation mobile suits, that nonetheless reflects how seriously Zeon's ground command in this theater treated the 08th Team as a threat worth committing dedicated armor assets against.
