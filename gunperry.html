Overview

The Gunperry is the Federation's answer to a problem Zeon's Gaw was built to solve for the opposing side: getting mobile suits to a forward position faster than they can walk there. A vertical-takeoff transport aircraft, it lets the Kojima Battalion redeploy its Gundam and GM complement across the Southeast Asia theater's difficult terrain without relying solely on ground movement through jungle and mountain that could take days to cross on foot.

Armament

  • Defensive Machine GunsLimited self-defense armament; not built as a combat platform
  • Mobile Suit Transport Bay/HardpointsPrimary function — rapid redeployment of mobile suit assets

Operational History

Gunperry transports supported the 08th MS Team's mobility across the Southeast Asia theater throughout the campaign, particularly for redeployments where speed mattered more than the element of surprise a ground approach might otherwise offer. Its role in this archive's holdings is consistently logistical rather than combat-direct.
