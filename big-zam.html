Overview

The Big Zam is among the largest and most heavily armed mobile weapons fielded by either side during the One Year War — a single-unit strategic asset rather than a production line, built around an all-direction energy shield and overwhelming particle weaponry. Its scale and cost meant it was never intended for routine deployment, reserved instead for the war's most decisive engagements.
