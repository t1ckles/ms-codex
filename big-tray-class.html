Overview

The Big Tray-class is a Federation heavy landing ship, built to move mobile suits, vehicles, and bulk equipment from sea to shore in theaters lacking developed port infrastructure. Its flat, low-profile cargo deck prioritizes capacity over speed or armament, making it a vital but unglamorous link in the Kojima Battalion's supply chain into Southeast Asia.

Armament

Operational History

Big Tray-class vessels supported the Kojima Battalion's heavy equipment resupply into the Southeast Asia theater throughout the campaign, consistent with the class's standard logistics role across Federation ground operations. Specific engagement records remain limited, reflecting the class's deliberately low combat profile.
