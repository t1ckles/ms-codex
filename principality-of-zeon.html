Overview

Sahalin Command refers to the Zeon detachment built around the Apsalus Project — a secret mobile armor program housed at a hidden mountain base in Southeast Asia and directed by Ginias Sahalin of the aristocratic Sahalin family. Rather than a conventional line unit, this command was structured around protecting a single strategic asset: a mobile armor intended to fly over the Earth Federation's Jaburo headquarters and destroy it outright, ending the One Year War's stalemate in Zeon's favor.

The detachment answered nominally to wider Zeon command, but in practice operated with considerable autonomy, a fact that allowed Ginias Sahalin to continue the project covertly even after Zeon high command formally ordered it halted following the defeat at Operation Odessa.
