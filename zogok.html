Overview

The Zogok is a lesser-fielded entry in Zeon's broader MSM amphibious assault line, sharing the family's core design emphasis on underwater mobility and integrated close-range weaponry over conventional handheld armament. Production and deployment numbers were comparatively limited relative to the more widely fielded Gogg and Z'Gok designs.
