Overview

The Acguy is Zeonic Company's answer to the same reactor-cooling problem that produced the rival Zimmad Gogg, built around shared design lessons from that earlier program and components borrowed from the established Zaku II line to control development cost. Its distinctive rounded silhouette minimizes underwater drag, and despite ranking among the largest mobile suits fielded by either side at the time, its heat signature while submerged is nearly untraceable — a quality Zeon exploited directly in infiltration operations against the Federation's Jaburo headquarters.

Operational History

This archive's primary record of Acguy deployment against the 08th MS Team is also one of the unit's costliest engagements: a close encounter with an Acguy resulted in the outright decapitation of Karen Joshua's Gundam Ground Type, an event significant enough that her subsequent field-repaired unit — fitted with a salvaged GM Ground Type head — earned her the lasting nickname "GM Head" among pilots outside her own team. Cross-reference the Gundam Ground Type unit dossier and Karen Joshua's personnel file for the full incident record.
