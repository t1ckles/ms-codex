Overview

The Federation's standard battlefield helicopter rounds out the Kojima Battalion's conventional air assets, providing reconnaissance and light fire support in situations that don't call for a mobile suit but exceed what ground patrols alone can manage. In dense jungle terrain, where line-of-sight reconnaissance is often impossible from the ground, helicopter overwatch is a meaningful force multiplier for an otherwise mobile-suit-centric Federation ground force.

Armament

  • Door-Mounted Machine GunStandard fit for escort and suppression duty
  • Light Rocket PodsMission-dependent, anti-personnel and light-vehicle role

Operational History

Battle Heli units provided reconnaissance and escort support for Kojima Battalion ground operations throughout the Southeast Asia campaign, a consistent if unglamorous presence in this archive's incidental records of base operations and convoy movement alongside the 08th MS Team's more heavily documented mobile suit engagements.
