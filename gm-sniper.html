Overview

As the Apsalus Project pushed the 08th MS Team toward longer-range engagements against a flying mobile armor target, the Kojima Battalion found itself needing a dedicated fire-support role the standard GM Ground Type wasn't built for. The GM Sniper answers that gap: a converted GM Ground Type re-tuned for long-range precision work, with improved sensor accuracy and an operating system purpose-built around its signature long-range beam rifle. Cooling equipment and power transmission were likewise retuned to manage the heat load of sustained high-output beam fire — without those changes, the rifle's power draw would have made extended engagements impractical.

Aside from its sensor, cooling, and fire-control modifications, the GM Sniper's underlying frame performance is identical to the standard GM Ground Type it's converted from.

Operational History

GM Sniper units were instrumental in the 08th MS Team's running engagement with the Apsalus Project, providing overwatch and long-range suppression no standard-issue ground unit in the battalion could match. Their defining moment came during the final siege of Zeon's mountain stronghold: a GM Sniper ambush nearly brought down the fully armed Apsalus III outright, an attack halted only because Capt. Isan Ryer wanted the mobile armor captured intact rather than destroyed. Cross-reference Operation Records, "Siege of the Mountain Stronghold."
