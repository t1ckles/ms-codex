Overview

A heavier-duty cousin to the 08th MS Team's organic Type 74 Hover Truck, the Hover Cargo Truck is built around bulk capacity rather than sensor equipment — moving fuel, spare parts, and ordnance to forward positions rather than serving as a forward reconnaissance platform. Like the lighter Hover Truck, it rides on a ground-effect skirt suited to the same difficult terrain its mobile suit complement operates in.

Armament

  • None StandardOccasionally fitted with a pintle-mounted machine gun for convoy self-defense

Operational History

Hover Cargo Trucks formed the backbone of the Kojima Battalion's forward resupply chain throughout the Southeast Asia campaign, keeping the 08th MS Team's Gundam and GM complement fueled and armed far from any proper depot infrastructure — a logistical reality this archive's broader unit dossiers reference repeatedly when describing the battalion's field-improvisation culture.
