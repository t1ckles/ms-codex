Overview

The Dora is Zeon's super-heavy siege gun, a strategic-scale artillery piece built for bombardment missions far beyond the scope of conventional field guns like the Magella Attack. Its scale and immobility mean it's reserved for fixed defensive emplacements or set-piece offensive operations rather than mobile field deployment, functioning as a strategic asset more than a tactical one.

Operational History

Reference to Dora-class ordnance within this archive's Southeast Asia holdings is tied to the theater's broader strategic context — the type's notable role in the war's large-scale ground operations, including Operation Odessa, made it a familiar strategic reference point for Federation officers operating in any major ground theater, the Kojima Battalion included, even where direct engagement records remain sparse.
