Overview

The Type 74 Hover Truck is the 08th MS Team's organic support vehicle — not a combat platform in its own right, but the backbone of the unit's field intelligence and logistics capability. Riding on a ground-effect hover skirt rather than wheels or treads, it's built for the same rough Southeast Asian terrain its mobile suit complement operates in, and its radar suite provides the early-warning coverage that lets the team's Gundams fight on something better than pure reaction time.

Operational History

The Hover Truck's radar coverage is a constant presence across nearly every 08th MS Team engagement on file in this archive, from routine jungle patrols through the running campaign against the Apsalus Project. Eledore Massis crewed the vehicle for longer than any other member of the team, joined partway through the campaign by Michel Ninorich as gunner and navigator. The truck and its crew came under direct fire on multiple recorded occasions, despite its rear-echelon role, including incidents serious enough to require field medical treatment for its crew. Cross-reference the personnel files for Eledore Massis and Michel Ninorich for their full service records.
