Background

Michel Ninorich transferred into the 08th MS Team at the same time as Shiro Amada, joining Eledore Massis as navigator and gunner aboard the unit's support hovertruck. Where Eledore handles driving and sensor work, Michel's role centers on coordination and direct fire support from the truck's mounted weapon — the two together forming the backbone of the 08th Team's non-mobile-suit support capability for the duration of the campaign.

Service Record

Routine Support Duty

For the majority of the campaign, Michel's contributions were primarily logistical and tactical support from the rear — coordinating hovertruck movement, managing communications between the truck and the team's mobile suit pilots, and providing supporting fire when engagements drew close enough to put the support vehicle itself at risk.

A Rare Cockpit Rotation

Michel saw rare direct mobile suit combat during the engagement against the Apsalus II, briefly trading positions with Terry Sanders Jr. to take a shot at the fleeing mobile armor — one of the only recorded instances of Michel piloting a Gundam unit rather than crewing the hovertruck. Cross-reference Operation Records, "Shivering Mountain: Apsalus II Intercept," for the full engagement account.

Personal Notes

Throughout the campaign, Michel maintained a long-distance correspondence with his girlfriend, known in surviving records only as "B.B.," who remained in the space colonies for the duration of the war. Following the war's end, Michel joined Kiki Rosita in refusing to accept Shiro Amada's presumed death, the two of them searching the region independently until they located Shiro and a pregnant Aina Sahalin living in a remote mountain cabin. This archive treats that account as an unconfirmed post-war report pending further declassification, consistent with its treatment of Shiro Amada's and Aina Sahalin's own files.
