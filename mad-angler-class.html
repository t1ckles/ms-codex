Overview

The Mad Angler-class is Zeon's dedicated attack submarine, built to operate independently of surface fleet support for extended covert transit and strike operations. In a theater as defined by waterways and coastline as Southeast Asia, submersible assets like the Mad Angler give Zeon a means of repositioning forces and supplies that Federation surface and air patrols can't easily track.

Operational History

Mad Angler-class submarines are documented within this archive's Southeast Asia holdings primarily in a logistics capacity, supporting Zeon's ability to resupply and reposition forces along the theater's extensive coastline without exposing surface transports to Federation patrols. Direct combat engagement records involving this class remain limited.
