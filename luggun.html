Overview

The Luggun is Zeon's counterpart to the Federation's light scout hovercraft, used for forward reconnaissance and screening ahead of larger Zeon ground movements. Like its Federation equivalents, it favors speed and a low profile over armor or firepower, intended to gather and relay intelligence rather than engage directly.

Operational History

Luggun reconnaissance craft are documented within this archive's Southeast Asia holdings as part of Zeon's forward screening operations ahead of the theater's larger mobile suit engagements, consistent with the type's standard scouting role across the wider war.
