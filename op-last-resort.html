EPISODE REFERENCE: "LAST RESORT" // ZEON MOUNTAIN STRONGHOLD, SOUTHEAST ASIA

Siege of the Mountain Stronghold

OUTCOME — STRONGHOLD TAKEN, HEAVY LOSSES
ENGAGEMENT MAP
REDACTED PENDING
DECLASSIFICATION
Theater
Zeon mountain stronghold, Southeast Asia
EFF Command
Capt. Isan Ryer, Kojima Battalion
EFF Losses
Multiple Guntanks, Gundam Ground Types, Gundam Ez8
Zeon Losses
Norris Packard (KIA), Ginias Sahalin (KIA), Apsalus III destroyed
Civilian Losses
Kerguelen hospital transport, destroyed

Objective

A full Federation assault on Zeon's hidden mountain base, intended to end the Southeast Asia campaign by eliminating the Apsalus Project at its source.

Engagement Summary

The assault opened with Col. Norris Packard's single-handed defense of a withdrawal corridor for the Zeon hospital transport Kerguelen, fought in a bombed-out city block against an 08th MS Team detachment screening a column of artillery Guntanks. Packard disabled two Gundam Ground Types and destroyed several Guntanks before being confronted by Shiro Amada, now piloting the rebuilt Gundam Ez8. Packard was killed in the resulting duel, having completed his immediate objective of eliminating the artillery threat to the Kerguelen — a sacrifice rendered moot when Capt. Isan Ryer ordered the transport destroyed regardless, killing the wounded Zeon personnel aboard.

Within the stronghold itself, Ginias Sahalin's mental state collapsed entirely. Defying orders to evacuate, he sealed the base's exits — killing Zeon officer Yuri Kellerne in the process — and executed his own development team rather than abandon the unfinished Apsalus III. He launched the completed mobile armor in an unauthorized strike against the besieging Federation forces, piloting it together with his sister Aina. When Aina broke with her brother's plan and attempted to stand down, Ginias shot her; she survived only because a pocket watch in her coat absorbed the round.

Shiro Amada deserted his post to reach Aina, an act for which Capt. Ryer ordered his summary execution on capture. Recovering Aina, Shiro fought alongside her in the Gundam Ez8 to bring down the Apsalus III, ending Ginias Sahalin's life and the Apsalus Project with it — though not before Ginias fired one final shot from the mobile armor's main weapon into the Federation's forward position. Both Shiro and Aina went down with the destroyed Apsalus III into the stronghold's volcanic caldera and were logged as missing, presumed killed.

Outcome

The stronghold was taken and the Apsalus Project terminated, formally closing out the 08th MS Team's defining operation of the Southeast Asia campaign. Federation losses were severe on both the personnel and materiel side, and the destruction of the Kerguelen under Capt. Ryer's orders remains a point of considerable controversy in surviving accounts of the battle.

Unconfirmed post-war reports, gathered separately by guerrilla liaison Kiki Rosita and support crewman Michel Ninorich, place both Shiro Amada and Aina Sahalin alive and living together in a remote mountain region, with Aina reportedly pregnant. This archive treats the matter as unresolved pending further declassification.
