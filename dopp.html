Overview

The Dopp is Zeon's standard light attack aircraft, a fast, lightly armed strike and escort platform used throughout the war for missions that didn't justify committing a heavier Gaw or a full mobile suit detachment. Its small size and speed make it well suited to escort duty for test flights and convoy protection, the role it's most directly documented in within this archive's Southeast Asia holdings.

Operational History

Col. Norris Packard flew a Dopp in an air-support role during one of Aina Sahalin's Apsalus test flights, escorting the experimental mobile armor against potential interception. When his aircraft sustained damage during the sortie, Packard was forced to break off and retreat, leaving Aina stranded — a difficult tactical call surviving accounts treat as a hard necessity rather than any failure of nerve on his part.
