Overview

The Dodai II is a hovering hardpoint sled that lets a mobile suit ride into position rather than walk — a stopgap answer to the same hard mobility ceiling that the Dom's foot-mounted hover jets were eventually developed to solve more elegantly. A mobile suit mounted on a Dodai II trades some independent combat flexibility for a substantial speed advantage when repositioning across open ground, useful for rapid-reaction deployment or hit-and-run tactics.

Operational History

Dodai II sleds are documented within this archive's Southeast Asia holdings as part of Zeon's broader ground-mobility doctrine for the theater, consistent with the type's standard use elsewhere in the war as a rapid-deployment platform for ground-combat mobile suits ahead of the Dom's wider fielding.
