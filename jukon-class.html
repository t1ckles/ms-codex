Overview

The Jukon-class is a dedicated underwater transport vessel, built to move supplies and personnel rather than fight — lightly armed if armed at all, and reliant on stealth and submersion rather than firepower for survival. In Zeon's broader underwater logistics network, it complements combat-focused classes like the Mad Angler by handling the bulk-transport role those vessels aren't optimized for.

Armament

  • Light Defensive ArmamentMinimal; the class relies on stealth over firepower
  • Cargo BayPrimary function — bulk supply and personnel transport

Operational History

Jukon-class vessels are documented in this archive's Southeast Asia holdings as part of Zeon's broader underwater resupply network for the theater, consistent with the class's standard transport role across the war. No direct combat engagement with the 08th MS Team is on file for this class.
