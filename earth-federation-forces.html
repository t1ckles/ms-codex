Overview

The Kojima Battalion was the Earth Federation Forces command structure responsible for the Southeast Asia theater during the One Year War — a guerrilla-style ground campaign fought far from the war's more publicized engagements, against entrenched Principality of Zeon forces protecting the secret Apsalus Project. The battalion's most documented sub-unit is the 08th Mobile Suit Team, whose service record makes up the bulk of this archive's operational holdings for this theater.
