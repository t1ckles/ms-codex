Overview

The Fanfan is a small fixed-wing transport used for personnel and light cargo movement between Kojima Battalion positions and rear-area logistics points. Unlike the VTOL Gunperry, it operates from improvised or fixed airstrips rather than vertical launch, making it suited to longer-range, lower-priority transit where speed of deployment matters less than fuel efficiency and capacity.

Operational History

Fanfan aircraft supported routine personnel and supply movement for the Kojima Battalion throughout the Southeast Asia campaign, a background logistics presence in this archive's holdings rather than a combat-relevant asset in its own right.
