Overview

The Gaw is one of Zeon's largest dedicated aircraft, a heavy VTOL platform built to project mobile suit and armor strength into theaters its forces couldn't otherwise reach quickly by ground. Mounted beneath a broad lifting-body airframe, it can carry mobile suits externally to a forward staging point and provide direct fire support once they've deployed — making it as much a flying logistics hub as a combat aircraft in its own right.

Operational History

Gaw heavy transports supported Zeon mobile suit deployments across the Southeast Asia theater throughout the 08th MS Team's campaign, providing the air-mobility backbone that let Zeon's relatively thin ground presence reposition forces to meet Federation incursions before they could consolidate. Direct engagements between the 08th Team and a Gaw in flight are on file but comparatively rare, reflecting the aircraft's primary role as a force multiplier rather than a front-line combatant.
