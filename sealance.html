Overview

The Sealance is purpose-built to solve a specific operational problem: getting amphibious mobile suits like the Gogg and Acguy to a theater of operations without exposing them, or a surface escort, to detection en route. Submerged transit lets Zeon stage amphibious assault forces close to a target coastline before surfacing to launch, denying the Federation the early warning a surface convoy would otherwise provide.

Operational History

Sealance-class vessels are documented within this archive's Southeast Asia holdings in connection with the theater's amphibious mobile suit activity, consistent with the class's standard role as a covert delivery platform for Gogg and Acguy units operating along the region's extensive coastline and waterways.
