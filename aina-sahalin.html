Background

Aina Sahalin is the younger sister of Ginias Sahalin and a member of Zeon's aristocratic Sahalin family. Raised within Zeon high society, she devoted herself to supporting her brother's ambitions to restore the family's standing, training as a mobile suit pilot in order to serve as lead test pilot for his Apsalus Project. Norris Packard, a longtime family servant, raised both Sahalin siblings and remained a surrogate father figure to Aina throughout her life, a relationship that informed much of her loyalty to the family's broader ambitions even as those ambitions grew increasingly unstable.

Aina first crossed paths with Shiro Amada during a chance encounter in a debris field in space, where the two fought to a standstill in damaged mobile weapons before being forced to cooperate to survive inside the wreck of a sunken Magellan-class battleship. Neither initially recognized the other as a combatant from the opposing side of the same regional war. Cross-reference Operation Records, "Distress Call in the Debris Field."

Service Record

Flight Testing the Apsalus Line

As the Apsalus Project's senior test pilot, Aina flew all three iterations of the mobile armor through their respective field trials. The Apsalus I's forced descent into Federation territory brought her into contact with Shiro Amada for the second time, though under circumstances that left both unaware they had already met. Cross-reference Operation Records, "Apsaras Program: First Contact."

The Shivering Mountain Incident

The Apsalus II's destruction during the Himalayan intercept now referred to as the Shivering Mountain incident marked a turning point in Aina's service record. Stranded for days with Shiro Amada in the wreckage after both their units went down, the two pilots — once again finding themselves on opposite sides of the same conflict — grew close under circumstances neither's chain of command would have condoned. Aina ultimately self-destructed the Apsalus II rather than allow Federation forces to capture it intact. Cross-reference Operation Records, "Shivering Mountain: Apsalus II Intercept."

Desertion

In the campaign's final days, as her brother's mental state deteriorated and the completed Apsalus III launched on an unauthorized strike against Federation forces, Aina ultimately broke with Ginias and sided with Shiro to bring the mobile armor down, ending the Apsalus Project at the cost of her brother's life. When she attempted to stand down during the engagement, Ginias shot her directly; she survived only because a pocket watch carried in her coat absorbed the round. Cross-reference Operation Records, "Siege of the Mountain Stronghold," for the complete engagement log.

Personal Notes

Aina is officially listed as missing in action following the destruction of the Apsalus III. Unconfirmed post-war reports, gathered independently by Kiki Rosita and Michel Ninorich, place a woman matching her description, pregnant, living with a man believed to be Shiro Amada in a remote mountain region; this archive treats the matter as unresolved pending further declassification.
