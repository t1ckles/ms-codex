Overview

The Z'Gok Commander Type upgrades the standard Z'Gok's sensor and communications suite for use as a squadron flagship unit, intended for higher-ranking pilots coordinating amphibious assault groups rather than line-unit duty. Its underlying combat performance matches the standard Z'Gok closely.

Armament

  • Mega Particle CannonIntegrated forearm mount, seawater-cooled

Operational History

Per general Zeon order-of-battle records, command-variant Z'Gok units led amphibious assault formations on fronts where Zeon's underwater forces saw concentrated use. No record on file connects this unit to the Southeast Asia front or the 08th MS Team's campaign.
